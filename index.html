what we do

Perception, Navigation and Exploration

We develop methodologies to tackle complex navigation problems by tightly coupling perception systems with a focus on the applications.

Learning by Practice

Students from the various ISAE courses are welcome to do their research projects with us.
Master students and trainees are also welcomes. Do not hesitate to openly candidate.

What are we?

A multi-department "Lab" of ISAE-SUPAERO

PNX lab is a way to regroup all the ISAE researchers from the robotics community. It's a way to share knowledge between students of all horizons.
